Traxettle is an expense splitter and shared expense tracker built for trips, roommates, events, group outings, and everyday bill splitting.

If you need an easy way to split bills, manage group expenses, track who paid, and settle up fairly, Traxettle helps you do it without spreadsheets, chat confusion, or manual calculations.

WHY PEOPLE USE TRAXETTLE
• Split expenses for trips, vacations, dinners, weddings, parties, and shared living
• Track group spending in one place
• See who owes whom with clear settlement flows
• Manage shared expenses across multiple people and multiple currencies
• Keep a clean history of active, settled, and closed events
