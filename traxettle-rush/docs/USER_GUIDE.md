# Traxettle User Guide - Complete Feature Overview

## 🎯 What is Traxettle?

Traxettle is your smart companion for splitting expenses with friends, family, and colleagues. Whether you're planning a vacation, sharing dinner costs, managing group projects, or organizing events - Traxettle makes it easy to track who paid what and who owes whom.

**Perfect for:**
- 🏖️ Vacation trips with multiple people
- 🍕 Restaurant bills and food delivery
- 🏠 Household expense sharing
- 🎉 Party and event planning
- 💼 Team lunches and office expenses
- 🎓 Student group projects and supplies

**Sign Up Options:**

**What You'll See:**
- **Welcome Message**: Personalized greeting with your name
- **Pro Status Badge**: Shows if you're using Free or Pro version
- **Theme Selector**: Choose between Light, Dark, and System themes

**Event Status Indicators:**
- 🟢 **Active**: Event is ongoing, expenses can be added
- 🟡 **Payment**: Settlement phase - time to settle up!

- ⚫ **Closed**: Event is archived and no longer active

## 💰 Managing Expenses

### Adding Expenses

**Quick Expense Entry:**
1. Open your event
2. Tap "Add Expense"
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: "Dinner at Seafood Restaurant"
   - **Amount**: $85.50
   - **Who Paid**: Select the person who actually paid
   - **Split Type**: How to divide the cost

### Split Types Explained

**🔄 Equal Split**
- Everyone pays the same amount
- Perfect for: Shared meals, group tickets, transportation
- Example: $120 dinner ÷ 4 people = $30 each

**⚖️ Ratio Split**
- Custom percentages for each person
- Perfect for: When some people should pay more/less
- Example: 60% / 40% split based on income or arrangement

**✏️ Custom Split**
- Specific amounts for each person
- Perfect for: Complex arrangements, partial participation
- Example: Person A pays $50, Person B pays $30, Person C pays $0

### Advanced Expense Features

**📸 Receipt Attachments**
- Take photos of receipts
- Upload bills and invoices
- Keep digital records for transparency

**🔒 Private Expenses**
- Mark expenses as private (only visible to you)
- Useful for personal items within group events
- Maintains privacy while tracking overall event costs

**🤝 "On Behalf Of"**
- Someone pays for multiple people
- Payer fronts money, others owe them
- Automatically calculates who owes whom

## 💸 Settlements Made Easy

### The Settlement Process

**1. Review Phase**
- All expenses are automatically calculated
- Review who owes whom
- Make any final adjustments
- Participants can approve or request changes

**2. Payment Phase**
- Clear payment instructions
- Multiple payment methods supported
- Track who has paid and who hasn't
- Send payment reminders

**3. Completion**
- Mark payments as received
- Event moves to "Settled" status
- Keep records for future reference

### Multi-Currency Magic (Pro Feature)

**How It Works:**
- Spend in local currency during your trip
- Settle in your preferred currency
- Automatic FX conversion at real rates
- No more complex calculations!

**Example Scenario:**
- Trip to Europe: Spend in EUR, GBP, and CHF
- Settlement: Everyone pays in their home currency (USD, INR, etc.)
- Traxettle handles all conversions automatically

### Payment Methods

Set up your preferred payment methods:
- **UPI**: Quick transfers (India)
- **Bank Transfer**: Traditional banking
- **PayPal**: International payments
- **Wise**: Low-cost international transfers
- **SWIFT**: Bank-to-bank transfers
- **Custom**: Any other method you prefer

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is Traxettle really free?**
A: Yes! Traxettle is free for up to 3 events. No ads, no hidden costs. Pro features require a subscription.

**Q: How do multi-currency settlements work?**
A: You spend in local currency during your trip, then settle in your preferred currency. Traxettle uses real exchange rates to convert everything automatically.

**Q: Can I use Traxettle without internet?**
A: Yes! The app works offline and syncs when you're back online.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never share your data with third parties.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What platforms support Traxettle?**
A: iOS, Android, and Web apps with full synchronization.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes! Pro users can export to Excel, PDF, or CSV formats.

**Q: How many people can join an event?**
A: There's no limit on participants per event.

**Q: Can I change the currency after creating an event?**
A: Yes, you can update event settings before settlement begins.

### Billing Questions

**Q: Can I cancel my Pro subscription?**

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**
