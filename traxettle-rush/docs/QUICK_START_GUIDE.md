# Traxettle Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 5-Minute Setup

### Step 1: Download & Sign Up (1 minute)
1. **Download Traxettle** from your app store
2. **Choose your sign-up method**:
   - 📧 Email + Password
   - 🔵 Google Sign-In
   - ✉️ Email Magic Link
3. **Enter your name** and you're ready!

### Step 2: Create Your First Event (2 minutes)
1. **Tap "Create Event"** on your dashboard
2. **Fill in the basics**:
   - Name: "Weekend Brunch"
   - Type: Event
   - Add your friends by email
3. **Set currency** and dates
4. **Tap "Create Event"** - Done!

### Step 3: Add Your First Expense (2 minutes)
1. **Open your new event**
2. **Tap "Add Expense"**
3. **Enter details**:
   - What: "Sunday Brunch"
   - Amount: $45.00
   - Who paid: Select yourself
   - Split: Equal between everyone
4. **Save** - Your expense is now tracked!

## 🎯 Most Common Uses

### 🍕 Splitting a Restaurant Bill
1. Create event: "Dinner at Italian Place"
2. Add everyone who was there
3. Add expense: Total bill $120
4. Choose "Equal split"
5. Done! Everyone owes $30

### ✈️ Weekend Trip
1. Create event: "Beach Weekend 2024"
2. Add all trip participants
3. Add expenses as they happen:
   - Hotel: $400 (split equally)
   - Gas: $60 (split by car usage)
   - Food: Various amounts
4. At the end, tap "Settle Up"
5. See exactly who owes whom

### 🏠 Roommate Expenses
1. Create monthly event: "March Expenses"
2. Add roommates
3. Track ongoing expenses:
   - Rent: Split by room size
   - Groceries: Who bought what
   - Utilities: Equal split
4. Settle at month-end

## 🔧 Essential Settings

### Profile Setup (30 seconds)
1. **Tap your avatar** in top-right
2. **Add photo** and verify email
3. **Set default currency** for your country
4. **Choose theme**: Light, Dark, or System

### Payment Methods (1 minute)
1. **Go to Profile → Payment Methods**
2. **Add your preferred methods**:
   - UPI (India)
   - Bank transfer
   - PayPal
   - Wise (international)
3. **Set as default** for easy settlements

## 🆘 Quick Help

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I change who paid for an expense?**
A: Yes! Just swipe right on the expense to edit.

**Q: What if someone doesn't have the app?**
A: You can invite them by email - they'll get a link to join.

**Q: Can I use it for business expenses?**
A: Absolutely! Perfect for team lunches and supplies.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes! Bank-level encryption and privacy-first design.

### Quick Fixes

**Problem: Can't find your event**
- Solution: Pull down to refresh, check the "All Events" tab

**Problem: Wrong split amount**
- Solution: Swipe right on expense, adjust splits, save

**Problem: Settlement seems wrong**
- Solution: Review all expenses, check for duplicates, recalculate
