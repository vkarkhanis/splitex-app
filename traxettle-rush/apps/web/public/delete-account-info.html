Delete Your Traxettle Account

Last updated: March 1, 2026

⚠️ Important: This action is permanent

Once you delete your account, there is no way to recover it or your data. Please make sure you have backed up any important information before proceeding.

What Will Be Deleted

When you delete your Traxettle account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile Information - Your email address, display name, and account settings
  • Events You Created - All events where you are the creator/admin
  • Expenses You Added - All expense entries you created across all events
  • Payment Methods - Your saved UPI IDs, bank accounts, PayPal, and Wise details
  • Group Memberships - Your participation in groups within events
  • Settlement Records - Your payment history and settlement references
  • Analytics Data - Your spending analytics and export history
  • App Preferences - Your theme settings, currency preferences, and other app configurations

What Will NOT Be Deleted

  • Events Created by Others - Events where you are a participant but not the creator will remain
  • Your Name in Others' Events - Your display name may remain visible in event histories for other participants
  • Payment Proof Images - Receipt images you uploaded may be retained for audit purposes as required by law

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the Traxettle mobile app:

1. Open Traxettle on your mobile device and sign in to your account
2. Tap the Menu (hamburger icon in the top-left corner of the Dashboard)
3. Select "Profile" from the menu options
4. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen
5. Tap "Delete Account" (red button below "Sign Out")
6. Confirm Deletion - Read the warning message carefully and tap "Delete" to confirm
7. Done! - Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted

Alternative: Contact Support

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email, you can contact us at:

traxettleapp@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • A clear request to delete your account
  • Any additional verification information if requested

Data Retention

After deletion:

  • Most of your data will be immediately removed from our active systems
  • Some data may be retained in backup systems for up to 30 days before permanent deletion
  • Certain data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (like fraud prevention)

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or this process, please contact our support team at traxettleapp@gmail.com.

Deleting your account is permanent. Please make sure you understand the consequences before proceeding.
